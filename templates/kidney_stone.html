Kidney Stone Detection

What are Kidney Stones?

Kidney stones are hard deposits made of minerals and salts that form inside your kidneys. They can affect any part of your urinary tract â€” from your kidneys to your bladder.

Common Causes of Kidney Stones:

  • Lack of water intake
  • Diet high in salt, sugar, or protein
  • Obesity
  • Medical conditions like hyperparathyroidism

Symptoms of Kidney Stones:

  • Severe pain in the back or abdomen
  • Painful urination
  • Cloudy or foul-smelling urine
  • Blood in urine

Prevention and Treatment:

  • Staying hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Reducing salt and protein in your diet
  • Medications to manage pain or help pass the stone
  • Surgery or other medical interventions in severe cases
